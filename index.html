Some common questions
were often asked

Why are my flowers wilting or losing freshness?

Flowers need fresh water, proper trimming, and a cool environment. Change the water daily and trim the stems at an angle to keep them fresh longer.

What causes petals to fade or discolor?

Direct sunlight, excessive heat, or lack of hydration can cause petals to lose their color. Keep your flowers in a cool, shaded area for longevity.

Why do my flowers have brown, crispy edges?

This is often due to dehydration or low humidity. Mist your flowers lightly and ensure they have enough water. Avoid placing them near air conditioners or heaters.

How do I select the perfect bouquet?

Consider the occasion, preferred colors, and flower meanings. Our experts can help you choose the best arrangement for any event.

How should I arrange flowers in a vase?

Trim stems at an angle, remove excess leaves, and place them in fresh water with flower food. Change the water every two days for lasting freshness.

Why are there tiny insects around my flowers?

Gnats and other small pests are attracted to stagnant water. Keep your vase clean, change the water regularly, and use a mild insect repellent if needed.
